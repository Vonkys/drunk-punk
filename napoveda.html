Help – 🎮 Drunk Punk

🧑‍🌾 How do I start farming?
Go to the "Grow Sh*t" section. First, open the shop (🛒 button), pick a seed (like wheat 🌾 or potatoes 🥔), and click "Buy." Then tap any free field tile to plant it. After a timer, the crop is ready to harvest. Easy.
🌾 What can I grow?
You can grow wheat, potatoes, barley, hops, grapes, fruits like apples, plums, and apricots. Each has a use – food, alcohol, or ingredients for more complex stuff.
🐄 How do animals work?
Buy animals in the Coop section. They need 1 wheat 🌾 every hour or they die. If you have auto-feed active (with Pi tokens), they won't starve. Animals produce items (eggs, milk, wool) after a few hours. Collect and use or sell.
🧺 Do products collect themselves?
Nope. Every product must be collected manually – nothing happens if you just stare at it. But hey, the dev is considering automation in the future (maybe for in-game tokens). So keep an eye on updates!
💩 Why is there poop?
Animals poop. If you don't clean it, they die. Clean the crap often. It’s farm life, baby.
🍺 How do I brew beer?
Buy a beer vat and boiler. You need wheat 🌾, hops 🍃 and time ⏳. First you ferment, then boil. Each beer has its own requirements.
🍷 How do I make wine?
Buy a wine press and vat. You need grapes 🍇 or other fruits and time ⏳. Press first, then ferment. Voilà – wine.
🔥 How do I distill spirits?
Get a distiller ⚗, fruit and time. First ferment the fruit, then distill it. Each spirit (rum, slivovitz, peach brandy) needs different mash.
💸 How do I earn money?
Sell your stuff in the Market or the Pub. You can sell items one by one, or do quests for bigger rewards. Don’t hoard – trade smart!
🧻 How does freeze feeding work?
Pay 1 Pi to freeze animal food for 24 hours. That means animals won't need wheat and won’t die. Buy peace of mind.
🥔 What does the fryer do?
Turns potatoes 🥔 into fries 🍟. Just buy the fryer and boom – you’re a grease king.
📈 How does XP work?
Every action gives XP: planting, harvesting, crafting, collecting animal products, fulfilling quests. More actions = more XP = flex on the leaderboard.
🪙 What are Pi Tokens for?
Use Pi to buy boosts: freeze feeding, expand field space, or buy game tokens in the Wallet. You earn more Pi by being active or through login rewards.
📅 What’s the daily login?
Click the Daily Login button every day to mark your streak. 30 days = reward in Pi. Miss a day? Back to day 1.
🤔 What’s coming next?
Tutorials, Cafeteria, Bakery, Dairy, Pizzeria, Mojitos, Weddings, and a Hotel for the elite! Stay tuned!
💡 Pro tips?

- Use your animals! They're the backbone.
- Quests = quick cash.
- Don’t forget to poop-scoop 💩.

Just chill and build your farming & booze empire. It’s a drunk life.
